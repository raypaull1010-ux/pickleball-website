Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of video should I send?

Competitive match play is best ‚Äî rec games, league matches, or tournaments. Drilling footage won't give me enough data to analyze patterns.

Should I choose 30 or 60 minutes?

More footage = better insights. 60 minutes gives me more data to identify patterns, but 30 minutes still provides a solid analysis. If you're serious about improving, go with 60.

What if I'm a beginner?

Video analysis works best for players 3.5+ who have consistent shot patterns to analyze. If you're newer to the game, you might benefit more from lessons first.

How do you send the results?

You'll receive everything via email: a written statistical report and a link to your video feedback walkthrough.

Can I submit doubles or singles?

Either works! Just let me know which format and who I'm analyzing (you or your partner).

What if I have questions after receiving my analysis?

Feel free to email or text me with follow-up questions. I'm happy to clarify anything in your report.
