Frequently Asked Questions

What if I can't submit my second video exactly at 2 weeks?

No problem! The program is flexible. Submit your follow-up video whenever you're ready ‚Äî slightly before or after the 2-week mark is fine.

What video should I send?

30 minutes of competitive match play (rec games against strong opponents, league matches, etc.). The more game-like, the better.

Can I do the drills without a partner?

Many drills require a partner, but I'll include solo wall drills when possible. I'll design your program based on your practice situation.

I already have a workout routine. Can I combine them?

Absolutely! The pickleball workouts are designed to complement your game, not replace your existing routine. We can discuss how to integrate them.

How is this different from a single video analysis?

A single analysis tells you what to work on. Level Up gives you the complete roadmap: analysis + drills + workouts + progress tracking over a full month.

Can I renew for another month?

Yes! Many players continue with additional months. Each month builds on your progress with fresh analysis and updated programs.

What's the health questionnaire for?

Complete and Elite packages include workout plans. The health questionnaire helps me customize exercises for your age, fitness level, and any injuries or limitations. It also includes a liability waiver required for workout programs.

What if I have injuries or limitations?

No problem! I'll modify exercises based on your health questionnaire. Whether it's bad knees, shoulder issues, or anything else, I'll provide alternatives that work for your body.
